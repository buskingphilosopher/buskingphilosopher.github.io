About

Welcome to a peculiar blog.

I am Resident Philosopher at Reference.Point where I typewrite outside on the steps every Saturday and talk to passersby about philosophy.

I ask them to think up any 5 words and I talk them through philosophical ideas related to those words while also typing them a page of philosophy they can walk away with.

Come find me from 12-6pm on Saturdays, or write to me with your own 5 words and I'll upload my response on this blog. Reach out to buskingphilosopher@gmail.com.

Thanks for visiting.
