The promise of political science was to understand politics better. Yet we still find ourselves prey to politicians avoiding the question in interviews.

Is there a solution to this? I think there is. Peter Bull famously noticed there were only 35 ways all politicians avoided questions. That's a manageable number. This website is my attempt to begin breaking down real interviews to show how Bull's typology can help us deal with this real, crucial political problem.

Disinformation isn't inevitable. We can keep on top of it and hold our politicians to account. This is how.
