How to Lie with Rhetoric

This webpage is named in the spirit of Darrell Huff's book How to Lie with Statistics which teaches how to spot when politicians lie by manipulating statistics.

The page is devoted to analysing rhetorical arguments, primarily through classical analysis of political speeches – a dying art but one I think offers invaluable insight into why politicians still find speeches an effective instrument for achieving power.

If we can understand what politicians understand about rhetoric, we won't be fooled so easily by their tricks. My hope is these analyses will help spread awareness and better shield the public from rhetorical sleight of hand.
