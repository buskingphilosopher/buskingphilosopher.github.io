Lewin's Lexicon

Admiral of the Fleet Lord Lewin KG was known to issue orders and convey situations by quoting Shakespeare — citing act, scene, and line. Recipients had to look up the reference to decode the message.

The direct-order signals (marked in red) are lesser-known lines that function as literal commands to the addressee. The situational signals (in green) draw a parallel between the scene's famous dramatic moment and the current naval circumstance.
