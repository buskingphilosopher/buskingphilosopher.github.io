How to Lie with Rhetoric

This webpage is named in the spirit of Darrell Huff's book How to Lie with Statistics which teaches how to spot when politicians lie by manipulating statistics.

The page is devoted to analysing any argument in whatever context, from trivial to philosophical. My aim is to develop a step-by-step methodology I have been building, bringing together techniques developed by philosophers and mathematicians, to take any argument, no matter how complicated or wordy, and reduce it to its logical structure to be subjected to validity testing and precise criticism.

Plato believed the task of the philosopher was to see through bullshit (not his wording). There is a lot of disinformation all the time and this procedure I demonstrate on this webpage can help us to sift strong arguments from weak ones. My hope is these analyses will help spread awareness and better shield the public from logical sleight of hand. And it illustrates we can be proactive about the proliferation of disinformation.
